# Product System

## Store Model

Purveyor of Curiosities sells rotating, mixed-category inventory recovered from:

- Estate cleanouts
- Storage auctions
- Rental turnovers
- Trash-out projects
- Local cleanup jobs
- Miscellaneous resale opportunities

Most inventory may be one-of-one. The system must clearly support single-quantity products, condition grading, local pickup, shipping availability, and honest item notes.

## Main Product Categories

### 1. Home & Everyday

Household goods, decor, kitchen items, sealed soaps, sealed lotions, candles, small appliances, storage items, and general everyday products.

### 2. Garage & Auto

Automotive accessories, trim pieces, detailing supplies, small parts, manuals, garage items, and non-safety-critical automotive finds.

Avoid at launch:
- Airbags
- SRS modules
- Seat belts
- Used tires
- Brake-critical parts
- Steering-critical parts
- Unknown safety electronics

### 3. Tools & Hardware

Hand tools, small power tools, hardware, fasteners, shop supplies, toolboxes, clamps, meters, and repair-related items.

### 4. Collectibles & Oddities

Vintage goods, unusual objects, antiques, decorative items, memorabilia, conversation pieces, and strange finds.

### 5. Electronics & Gadgets

Small electronics, accessories, cables, chargers, speakers, gadgets, and devices.

All electronics should be marked:
- Tested
- Powers On
- Untested
- For Parts / Repair

### 6. Local Pickup Deals

Large, heavy, fragile, awkward, or low-margin items that are not worth shipping.

### 7. Sold Archive

Previously sold items used for social proof, portfolio value, and showing that inventory moves.

## Condition Grades

Use these consistently:

### New / Sealed

Factory sealed or unused with original packaging.

### Open Box

Opened but appears unused or complete.

### Excellent Used

Very clean used condition with minimal wear.

### Good Used

Normal signs of use but still solid and presentable.

### Fair Used

Visible wear, scratches, missing minor pieces, or cosmetic flaws.

### For Parts / Repair

Not fully working, damaged, incomplete, or sold for repair/parts only.

### Untested

Not tested. Buyer should assume unknown working condition.

### Local Pickup Only

Item is not available for shipping.

## Product Description Template

Use this format for product listings:

### Overview

Short plain-English explanation of what the item is.

### Condition

State the condition grade and explain visible wear or known issues.

### Included

List what comes with the item.

### Testing

Explain whether it was tested, visually inspected, powered on, or untested.

### Source

Briefly explain the find source if appropriate.

### Shipping / Pickup

State whether the item can be shipped or is local pickup only.
