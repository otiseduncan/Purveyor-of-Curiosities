✦How It Works

Found, Sorted, Documented, Claimed

The workflow is part of the trust system: no mystery listings, no vague condition claims, and no pretending every item is perfect.

▣

Risk-aware product rules

Personal-care items must be sealed, labeled, and not expired. Auto parts stay non-safety-critical at launch. Untested electronics are marked clearly.

01
Process

We Find It

Items come from estate cleanouts, storage auctions, rental turnovers, trash-out projects, and local recovery jobs.

02
Process

We Sort It

Each item is reviewed, cleaned when appropriate, photographed, categorized, and assigned condition notes.

03
Process

We List It Clearly

Listings include photos, condition grades, testing notes, included items, and shipping or pickup information.

04
Process

You Claim It

Many products are one-of-one. If you see something you want, grab it before it disappears.

One Available

When It's Gone, It's Gone.

Most inventory is recovered in small quantities or as single finds. We do not always know what will come in next, and we usually cannot restock the same item.
